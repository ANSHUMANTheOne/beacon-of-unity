Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information on the Beacon of Unity platform.

Last updated: June 2025
01 — Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Beacon of Unity ("we", "us", or "our") is built on trust. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of every member of our verified UAE community platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our services.

By using Beacon of Unity, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

⚠️ We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to operate and improve the Beacon of Unity platform.
02 — Information We Collect

What We Collect

To provide a verified, trust-first community experience, we collect:

  • Identity Information: Your full legal name and Emirates ID (submitted during verification)
  • Account Information: Email address, profile details, and authentication data
  • Activity Data: Help requests, posts, responses, Unity Points earned, and community interactions
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and usage logs
  • Communications: Messages you send through our platform
03 — Identity Verification

Emirates ID & Selfie Verification

Beacon of Unity requires a live selfie with a physical Emirates ID for membership verification. This is our most sensitive data category, and we treat it with the highest level of protection:

  • Your ID photo is reviewed only by human admins — never by automated systems alone
  • Verification photos are deleted after approval or rejection — we do not retain them
  • We do not share ID images with any third party under any circumstances
  • Rejection decisions are stored only as a flag (no personal image retained)
🛡️ Verification data is handled under strict internal protocols. Only designated admin accounts can access submitted ID reviews.
04 — How We Use Your Data

Purpose of Data Collection

We use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and maintain a trusted, scam-free community
  • Operate and improve platform features (help requests, lost & found, community hub)
  • Calculate and display Unity Points and leaderboard rankings
  • Send important account notifications (approval, rejection, updates)
  • Investigate and act on reported misconduct or abuse
  • Comply with applicable UAE laws and regulations
05 — Data Storage & Security

How We Protect Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, which complies with international security standards. We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • Firestore security rules ensuring only authenticated users access their own data
  • Admin-only access controls for sensitive review data
  • Regular security audits of our platform

While we take every precaution, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

06 — Your Rights

Your Data Rights

As a member of Beacon of Unity, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Object to certain uses of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at anshuman@beaconofunity.com. We will respond within 30 days.
