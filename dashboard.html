Platform Updates

Latest news from the Beacon of Unity team

📱 New March 2026

Official Android App Launched

Beacon of Unity is now available as a native Android app! The app wraps the full platform in a polished WebView with Google Sign-In support, status bar theming, and a splash screen. Download it to get the full experience natively on your phone. 🎉

🔔 Feature March 2026

Push Notifications

The Android app now delivers real-time push notifications for new chat messages, help requests posted in your region, and lost & found reports. Notifications work even when the app is closed — you'll never miss something important in your community.

⚡ Improvement March 2026

Real-Time Chat Unread Indicator

The red unread glow on active conversations now updates live — no reload needed. If someone messages you while the site is open, the glow appears instantly. Opening a chat also clears the indicator immediately.

✨ Feature March 2026

Volunteer Rank System Launched

Earn points with every resolved chat and climb through volunteer ranks — from Newcomer all the way to Guardian of Unity. Check your rank on the Leaderboard!

🟢 Live March 2025

Pilot Phase Now Live!

The pilot phase of the Beacon of Unity program is currently ongoing. We're testing, learning, and growing together — and we couldn't do it without you. Thank you for being a part of this! 🌟

📱 Improvement March 2025

Mobile Browser Compatibility

Fixed a critical issue where news would not load on mobile browsers (Android Chrome, iOS Safari). The root cause was an unsupported AbortSignal.timeout() API — replaced with a universal AbortController + setTimeout approach that works across all devices and browsers.

🎨 Design March 2025

Logout Button Redesign

The logout button is now a red circular FAB fixed at the bottom-left of the screen. It expands into a pill on hover, revealing the "Sign Out" label with a smooth animation.

💬 Improvement March 2025

iMessage-Style Chat Bubbles

The chat UI was redesigned with Apple iMessage-style bubbles — consecutive messages from the same sender are grouped together, timestamps appear beside the bubble on hover (left for you, right for them), and the overall feel is cleaner and more modern.

🎨 Design March 2025

Beacon of Unity Logo Added

The official Beacon of Unity logo now appears next to the platform name in the sidebar.

⚡ Performance March 2025

News Loads Faster on All Devices

News now pre-fetches as soon as you log in so it's ready before you even open the tab. Al Jazeera loads first and renders immediately, with other sources merging in the background. Results are cached locally so returning visitors see news instantly on every device.

📰 Feature March 2025

Reliable News Tab Launched

A brand new Reliable News tab is now live, pulling real-time stories from Al Jazeera, BBC News, NPR News, and Times of India. Filter by source or topic — War & Conflict, Sports, Business, Tech, Health, UAE Local, India, Politics, Entertainment, and Environment.

💬 Feature March 2025

Chat Message Translation

Every chat message now has a translate button. Hover over any message and tap the translate icon to instantly translate it into your selected language using the MyMemory API.

🌐 Improvement March 2025

Language Switcher Added

You can now switch the platform between English, Arabic (عر), and Hindi (हिं) using the language switcher in the sidebar. Google Translate powers the full-page translation instantly.

✨ Feature March 2025

Lost & Found Tab

Community members can now post lost or found items with a location and category. Posts are region-filtered so you only see what's relevant to your area.

✨ Feature March 2025

Volunteer Opportunities Tab

Community members can post and browse volunteer requests with urgency levels, categories, and locations. Volunteers can sign up directly from the card and earn points toward their rank.

🎨 Design March 2025

Dark Sidebar & Navigation Polish

The sidebar received a full visual overhaul — deep navy background (#0f172a), sky-blue active states, Segoe UI typography, smooth hover transitions, and a consistent icon layout across all nav items.
