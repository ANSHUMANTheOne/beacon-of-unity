What is Beacon of Unity?

Beacon of Unity is a verified community platform built exclusively for UAE residents — connecting neighbours through trust, Emirates ID verification, and a shared commitment to helping one another across every emirate.

Community Help Requests

Residents post help requests categorised by type — groceries, tech, transport, and more. Every card shows the requester's distance from you, time remaining, and how many volunteers have responded. All interactions are between Emirates ID-verified members.

Lost & Found Board

A dedicated space for reporting and recovering lost items. Posts include photos, FOUND or LOST status tags, and exact Emirate locations like Mega Mall or Abu Shagara. A direct "Contact User" button connects owners and finders — no middlemen, no delays.

Volunteer Opportunities

A curated list of community service events sourced from Volunteers.ae — beach cleanups, food drives, tree planting, and tutoring sessions. Each listing shows the event date, location, and a direct registration link so you can act immediately.

Reliable News

A news aggregator pulling trusted stories from Khaleej Times, Gulf News, Al Jazeera, and BBC. Filter by source or topic — War & Conflict, Tech, Environment — so you stay informed on what matters most to your community, without misinformation.

Gamification & Rewards

Every helpful act earns Unity Points. Unlock 37 achievement badges across Helping and Requests milestones — from "First Step" to "10x Helper." Redeem points for profile gradient skins like Ocean, Mint, and Sunset on the public leaderboard.

Profile, Thank You Wall & More

Your verified name is permanent. Track your volunteer rank, resolved chats, average rating, and badge count on your profile. Post public appreciation notes on the Thank You Wall. Change your Emirate or update your live GPS location anytime from the dashboard.
